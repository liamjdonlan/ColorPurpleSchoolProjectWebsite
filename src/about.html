Our Founder

Celie

Celie started making pants during a very difficult period of her life. She had learned that learned that her husband had kept all news of her sister from her for many years. Therefore, Celie started making pants in order to focus on something other than her frustration. Not only did making pants lessen Celie’s anger, they had numerous other benefits on her life. Despite living a very difficult life living with an abusive stepfather, abusive husband, and being separated from her sister for many years, making pants provided Celie with a source of happiness. Celie loved making pants and would make pair after pair after pair. Eventually, Celie turned her hobby into a business and was able to become financially independent. Furthermore, Celie’s pants gave her greater self confidence and allowed her to support other people as well.
