Our Mission

Empowering everyone!

The goal of Folkspants limited is to give freedom and opportunity to other people. We achieve this in a variety of ways. First we break gender stereotypes by selling unisex pants that can be worn by people of any gender; this allows people to be more free by choosing what they would want to wear instead of what society believes they should wear. Second, we provide jobs to women in a time when few were available and allow them to be financially independent. Third, our pants are custom made to allow people to live their lives more freely. For example, they have introduced a new line of pants for pregnant women allowing them to live their lives uninhibited. If you want to see more specific examples of how we enable people to be free in their lives, see our glowing reviews on the home page.
